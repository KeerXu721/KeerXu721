Brandeis Running Club

  • I love running and really enjoy the overwhelming sense of euphoria, and it is one of my favorite sports. So I decided to establish a Running Club to find more like-minded students who also enjoy running. During the first semester, we successfully recruited more than 100 members. We meet twice a week to run for about 5km. I believe that everyone can run 5km, and everyone will love running with us!
